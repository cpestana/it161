Client

Client's name / final decision maker Mark Anderson
Client's business name Mark Anderson Plaster and Stucco
Redesign or brand new site? Brand new site
Type of website Identity and marketing
Client's goal The client's goal is to have an online presence to show their identity and business. It will also be used for marketing purposes to gain more attention and get more business with pictures of their work -- almost like a portfolio.

Audience

Audience Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Customers: Prospective clients who were looking for this kind of business and found their way to Mark's website. Most of his clients are large business but many users would have a mobile phone, so always make it available for all technology.
  • Needs contact information or to be able to submit a form to be contacted or to get a quote.
  • Needs to see Examples of work and skills.
  • Needs to be able to easily navigate and use the site.
  • Contact form or page for Mark on the site.
  • Photos of previous jobs up on the site.
  • CV or resume page to show experience.
  • Welcoming and easy to use homepage.
Past Customers: These are Mark's prior clients that are looking for future service and to leave a review for past jobs. These users would most likely use laptop and mobile phone, similar to prospective clients.
  • Needs to be able to submit a review from prior job done.
  • Needs to be able to contact Mark or submit a form to be contacted to get a quote
  • Needs to be able to navigate and use site easily.
  • Contact page or form for Mark on site.
  • Review form to submit a review.
  • Welcoming and easy to use homepage.
Prospective Employees: These viewers will be using the site specifically to see if there are any job openings coming up. I would say that most employees would use a laptop, but I'm sure some would use the phone as well.
  • Needs to be able to see if job site is coming up.
  • Needs to be abe to use the site easily.
  • Get contact information for Mark.
  • Page for employees posting jobs and needed workers coming up.
  • Easy to use navigation on homepage
  • Contact page or form for Mark on site.
